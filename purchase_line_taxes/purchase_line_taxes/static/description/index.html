Purchase Line Taxes

Bulk Tax Management for Purchase Orders

Key Features

  • Apply taxes to all RFQ/PO lines at once
  • Save time on bulk tax updates
  • Only shows purchase taxes
  • Works with Odoo 16 Enterprise

How It Works

Simply select the taxes you want to apply in the "Purchase Taxes" field at the purchase order header level, and all lines will automatically be updated with those taxes.

Compatible with Odoo 16 Enterprise

Designed specifically for Odoo 16 Enterprise and tested on odoo.sh
