Merge Purchase Order

Use to merge two or more purchase orders.

These module provide an overview of the different merge types and their effects on the quotations within your module. Users can choose the appropriate merge type based on their specific requirements and workflow.

  • New Cancel
    • This creates a new quotation and simultaneously cancels all of the selected quotations. This means that existing quotations will be replaced by a new one, and the canceled quotations will no longer be considered for further processing.

  • New Delete
    • This option is useful if you want to replace existing quotations with a new one and remove the old quotations entirely from the system.

  • Merge Cancel
    • This useful to contents of the selected quotations are combined into one quotation, and the other quotations are canceled. It helps in consolidating multiple quotations into a single one while canceling the others.

  • Merge Delete
    • This useful to contents of the selected quotations are combined into one quotation,and other quotations are not canceled but deleted.
